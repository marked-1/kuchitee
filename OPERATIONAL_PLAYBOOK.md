## Part 2: Pricing Strategy

### Pricing Framework

KuchiTee uses a tiered pricing strategy based on market, niche, and production cost.

**Pricing Formula:**
```
Retail Price = (Production Cost + Fulfillment Cost + Marketing Cost) × Markup (2.5-3.5x)
```

**Cost Breakdown:**
- Production Cost: $4-7 USD per unit
- Fulfillment Cost: $1-2 USD per unit (shipping, packaging, handling)
- Marketing Cost: $1-3 USD per unit (amortized across sales)
- Total Cost: $6-12 USD per unit
- Target Retail Price: $15-35 USD per unit

---

### Market-Specific Pricing

**Market 1: Latin America (Brazil, Mexico)**
- Retail Price: $18-26 USD
- Local Currency: R$ 90-130 BRL (Brazil), $300-400 MXN (Mexico)
- Markup: 3x cost
- Rationale: Growing middle class, willing to pay premium for quality

**Market 2: Southeast Asia (Philippines, Vietnam)**
- Retail Price: $12-18 USD
- Local Currency: ₱600-900 PHP (Philippines), ₫250-400K VND (Vietnam)
- Markup: 2.5x cost
- Rationale: Price-sensitive market, high volume strategy

**Market 3: Africa (South Africa, Nigeria, Kenya)**
- Retail Price: $12-18 USD
- Local Currency: R150-250 ZAR (South Africa), ₦3,000-5,000 NGN (Nigeria), KES 1,200-1,800 (Kenya)
- Markup: 2.5x cost
- Rationale: Emerging market, affordability important for volume

**Market 4: India (Tier 2-3 Cities)**
- Retail Price: $10-16 USD
- Local Currency: ₹400-700 INR
- Markup: 2.5x cost
- Rationale: Price-sensitive market, high volume strategy, large population

**Market 5: Middle East (Future)**
- Retail Price: $20-30 USD
- Local Currency: SAR 75-110 (Saudi Arabia), AED 70-110 (UAE)
- Markup: 3.5x cost
- Rationale: Affluent market, premium positioning

### Currency Management

**Strategy:**
- Price all products in USD on Etsy and website
- Use local currency for marketing materials
- Convert prices daily using mid-market rates
- Build 5-10% buffer for currency fluctuations

**Currency Conversion Process:**
1. Establish base USD price
2. Convert to local currency using daily mid-market rate
3. Round to nearest convenient amount
4. Add 5% buffer for fluctuations
5. Update prices weekly or as needed

**Example Conversion (Anime Design):**
- Base USD Price: $15
- Conversion Rate: 1 USD = 83 INR
- Local Price: 15 × 83 = ₹1,245
- With 5% Buffer: ₹1,310
- Final Price: ₹1,299 (rounded for convenience)

## Part 3: Fulfillment Strategy

### Fulfillment Process

**Step 1: Order Received (Etsy/Website)**
- Customer places order
- Payment processed automatically
- Order confirmation sent to customer
- Order details forwarded to vendor

**Step 2: Production (Days 1-7)**
- Vendor receives order details
- Design printed on selected size/color
- Quality check performed
- Item packaged

**Step 3: Shipping (Days 8-14)**
- Package picked up from vendor
- Tracking number generated
- Shipping label applied
- Package shipped to customer

**Step 4: Delivery (Days 15-30)**
- Package in transit
- Customer receives tracking updates
- Package delivered to customer address
- Delivery confirmation

**Step 5: Post-Delivery (Days 31+)**
- Customer receives item
- Customer satisfaction survey sent
- Returns/exchanges processed if needed

### Returns & Exchanges

**Return Policy:**
- 30-day return window
- Full refund or exchange
- Free return shipping (up to $10)
- Customer covers excess shipping costs

**Return Process:**
1. Customer initiates return within 30 days
2. Provide return shipping label
3. Customer ships item back
4. Inspect item upon receipt
5. Process refund or send replacement
6. Provide tracking for refund/replacement

**Defect Handling:**
- If defective, provide immediate replacement
- No return shipping required
- Ship replacement within 3 days
- Track defect rate (target: <2%)

## Part 4: Quality Assurance

### Quality Standards

**Fabric Quality:**
- 100% cotton or cotton blend
- Soft, comfortable feel
- Durable for repeated washing
- No pilling or fading after 10+ washes

**Print Quality:**
- Sharp, clear design
- Accurate color reproduction
- No bleeding or smudging
- Durable for 50+ washes

**Sizing Accuracy:**
- Measurements within ±1 inch
- Consistent across all sizes
- True to size (not oversized/undersized)
- Comfortable fit for intended style

**Stitching Quality:**
- Straight, even stitching
- No loose threads
- Reinforced seams
- Professional finish

---

### Quality Control Process

**Pre-Production:**
- Review design files for quality
- Verify color specifications
- Confirm sizing requirements
- Approve vendor sample

**During Production:**
- Random sampling (10% of orders)
- Visual inspection of prints
- Check stitching and seams
- Verify sizing

**Post-Production:**
- 100% inspection of sample orders
- Customer feedback collection
- Defect rate tracking
- Continuous improvement

**Defect Rate Targets:**
- Acceptable defect rate: <2%
- Target defect rate: <1%
- Zero tolerance for major defects
- Immediate replacement for defects

## Part 6: Risk Management

### Operational Risks

**Risk 1: Vendor Quality Issues**
- Mitigation: Regular quality checks, backup vendors, clear specifications
- Response: Immediate replacement, vendor communication, improvement plan

**Risk 2: Shipping Delays**
- Mitigation: Multiple carriers, regional fulfillment, buffer inventory
- Response: Proactive customer communication, expedited shipping, refund if needed

**Risk 3: Demand Fluctuations**
- Mitigation: Flexible production, inventory management, demand forecasting
- Response: Adjust production, promotional pricing, design updates

**Risk 4: Currency Fluctuations**
- Mitigation: Price in USD, 5-10% buffer, regular price reviews
- Response: Adjust prices, negotiate with vendors, hedging strategies

**Risk 5: Competition**
- Mitigation: Focus on niches, community building, quality emphasis
- Response: Design innovation, influencer partnerships, customer loyalty

---

### Financial Risks

**Risk 1: Low Conversion Rate**
- Mitigation: A/B testing, product optimization, customer feedback
- Response: Redesign products, improve marketing, price adjustments

**Risk 2: High Customer Acquisition Cost**
- Mitigation: Organic growth, influencer partnerships, referral program
- Response: Adjust marketing strategy, focus on retention, community building

**Risk 3: Inventory Obsolescence**
- Mitigation: Demand forecasting, seasonal adjustments, design rotation
- Response: Clearance sales, design updates, inventory write-offs
