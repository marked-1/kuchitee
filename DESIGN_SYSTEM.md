# KuchiTee Design System

## Brand Philosophy
**KuchiTee** is a premium streetwear brand targeting 18-35 year-olds in niche interest communities worldwide. The design philosophy emphasizes bold, sarcastic, edgy, artistic, and premium aesthetics—NOT budget fast fashion.

## Color Palette
- **Primary Background**: Deep black (#0a0a0a) - premium, sophisticated
- **Accent Colors**: 
  - Red (#ef4444) - energy, passion
  - Gold (#fbbf24) - premium, luxury
  - Neon Blue (#0ea5e9) - tech, modern
  - Neon Green (#22c55e) - sci-fi, cyberpunk
  - White (#ffffff) - contrast, clarity
  - Pink (#ec4899) - women's niche, bold

## Typography
- **Display Font**: Bold, condensed serif for headlines (e.g., "VELOCITY", "SINGULARITY")
- **Body Font**: Clean sans-serif for descriptions (e.g., Poppins, Inter)
- **Accent Font**: Hand-lettered or script for feminine/artistic sections

## Layout Principles
1. **Dark Premium Aesthetic**: Solid black or very dark grey backgrounds
2. **High Contrast**: White, red, pink, gold, sky blue on black base
3. **Oversized Typography**: Large, bold, centered chest/back placement
4. **Minimalist Approach**: Clean, punch in simplicity
5. **Artistic Mood**: Premium, bold, NOT budget

## 8 Niches Design Guide

### 1. Biking
- **Sub-Themes**: Road biking, mountain biking, motorbike culture, speed, freedom
- **Design Style**: Bold graphic, speed lines, bike silhouettes, action-focused
- **Colors**: Black, red, white, chrome
- **Typography**: Bold serif / block condensed

### 2. Sarcasm
- **Sub-Themes**: Witty quotes, dry humor, anti-social, deadpan comedy
- **Design Style**: Minimal text-based, clean, punch in simplicity
- **Colors**: Black/white, muted tones
- **Typography**: Condensed sans-serif, impact

### 3. Women's Sarcasm
- **Sub-Themes**: Feminist humor, badass women quotes, girl-power edge
- **Design Style**: Edgy feminine, floral + bold contrast, attitude
- **Colors**: Black, pink, gold
- **Typography**: Mix serif + hand-lettering

### 4. Motivational / Emotional
- **Sub-Themes**: Inspirational quotes, mental health awareness, grind culture, resilience
- **Design Style**: Clean, impactful typography, minimal distractions
- **Colors**: Black/white, single accent color
- **Typography**: Strong display fonts, all-caps

### 5. Sci-Fi
- **Sub-Themes**: Space, AI, cyberpunk, dystopia, robots, futurism, Virtual Reality
- **Design Style**: Dark, glitchy, space-grade, layered textures
- **Colors**: Black, neon blue/green, purple, white
- **Typography**: Futuristic mono / tech fonts

### 6. Custom Superheroes
- **Sub-Themes**: Original characters (NO copyright), anti-hero archetypes, urban warriors
- **Design Style**: Comic-book line art, action scenes, power poses
- **Colors**: Black, red, gold, deep blue
- **Typography**: Bold comic fonts, speech-bubble style

### 7. Solo / Solitude
- **Sub-Themes**: Lone wolf, introvert culture, solo travel, self-reliance
- **Design Style**: Minimal, moody, atmospheric, wide open spaces
- **Colors**: Dark navy, grey, white, muted earth
- **Typography**: Thin modern fonts, lower case

### 8. Mountains
- **Sub-Themes**: Hiking, altitude culture, nature, adventure, summit vibes
- **Design Style**: Vintage outdoor poster, line art silhouettes
- **Colors**: Earth tones, dark green, white, burnt orange
- **Typography**: Distressed/vintage, hand-drawn feel

## Mockup Presentation Style
- **Background**: Dark / minimalist — solid black or very dark grey preferred
- **Lighting**: Angled to show fabric texture depth — gives premium, tactile feel
- **Presentation**: Ghost mannequin OR flat-lay (no live model required)
- **Design Placement**: Large centered chest placement OR large back placement
- **Feel / Mood**: Premium streetwear, artistic, bold — NOT budget fast fashion
- **Reference Brand**: mydesignation — women's oversized tees as style reference
- **Fit Style**: Oversized / boxy fit — dropped shoulders, heavy ribbed crewneck
- **Color Palette**: High-contrast on black base: white, red, pink, gold, sky blue
