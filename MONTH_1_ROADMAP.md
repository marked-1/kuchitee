## Risk Mitigation

### Potential Risks & Solutions

**Risk 1: Low Initial Sales**
- Solution: Increase content frequency, optimize Etsy SEO, run targeted ads (if budget allows)

**Risk 2: Design Not Resonating**
- Solution: Analyze niche trends, create more designs in top-performing niches, adjust design style

**Risk 3: High Supplier Costs**
- Solution: Negotiate bulk pricing, explore alternative suppliers, optimize design for print efficiency

**Risk 4: Low Social Media Engagement**
- Solution: Increase posting frequency, engage with niche communities, collaborate with micro-influencers

**Risk 5: Website Traffic Not Converting**
- Solution: Improve product pages, add customer testimonials, optimize checkout process, run retargeting campaigns
