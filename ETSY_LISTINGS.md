## Etsy Launch Strategy

**Week 1-2**: List 25-30 products (3-4 per niche)
**Week 3-4**: List remaining 20-25 products (3-4 per niche)

**SEO Optimization Tips**:
- Use long-tail keywords (e.g., "premium oversized motorcycle tee" instead of just "motorcycle shirt")
- Include niche-specific terms (e.g., "grind culture", "cyberpunk", "feminist")
- Add trending terms (e.g., "streetwear", "graphic tee", "oversized fit")
- Monitor Etsy search bar autocomplete for trending keywords
- Use all 13 tags strategically with mix of broad and specific terms
- Update listings based on search performance weekly

**Pricing Strategy**:
- Base price: $15-$18 for most designs
- Premium designs (back print, complex graphics): $19-$22
- Competitive with similar streetwear on Etsy
- Adjust based on niche performance

**Promotion**:
- Feature new designs on shop homepage
- Cross-link related niches
- Offer bundle deals (e.g., "Buy 2, Get 10% Off")
- Use Etsy Ads for top 5 performing designs
