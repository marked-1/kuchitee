## Reel Ideas (3/week)

### Reel 1: Niche Showcase
- Show all 8 designs with quick transitions

- Upbeat music with trending audio
- Call-to-action: "Which one are you?"

- Time-lapse of design creation
- Show final product on model/mockup
- Trending music
- Call-to-action: "Shop now"

- Different styling options
- Trending music
- Call-to-action: "Get yours"

## Posting Schedule

**Daily Posts**: 7 AM (India time) or 7 PM (US time)
**Reels**: Monday, Wednesday, Friday at 7 AM
**Stories**: 2-3x daily (morning, afternoon, evening)
**Engagement**: Respond to comments within 2 hours

---

## Engagement Strategy

### Community Building
- Respond to all comments within 2 hours
- Like and comment on posts from niche communities
- Follow relevant accounts (bikers, introverts, fitness enthusiasts, etc.)
- Engage with trending content in each niche

### Collaboration Opportunities
- Partner with micro-influencers in each niche (100-10k followers)
- Send free products for honest reviews
- Feature customer content
- Collaborate on Reels with other creators

### Call-to-Actions
- "Shop now" with link in bio
- "Comment your niche"
- "Tag someone who needs this"
- "Which design are you getting?"
- "Share your KuchiTee moment"

**Week 1**: 100-200 followers, 50-100 likes per post
**Week 2**: 200-400 followers, 100-200 likes per post
**Week 3**: 400-700 followers, 150-300 likes per post
