# KuchiTee Dashboard & Tracking System

## Dashboard Overview

The KuchiTee Dashboard is the central hub for tracking all business metrics across all channels. It provides real-time visibility into design performance, sales, engagement, and revenue.

---

## Key Metrics to Track

### Design Metrics
- **Designs Created**: Total designs generated (Target: 10+ by end of Week 1)
- **Designs Pending**: Designs awaiting supplier approval
- **Designs Live**: Designs currently listed and selling
- **Design Performance**: Views, clicks, and sales per design
- **Top Performers**: Designs with highest engagement/sales
- **Niche Performance**: Which niches are performing best

### Sales Metrics
- **Total Orders**: Cumulative orders across all channels
- **Revenue**: Total revenue generated (India ₹ + Global $)
- **Average Order Value**: Revenue / Orders
- **Orders by Channel**: Etsy, Instagram, Facebook, Website, Pinterest
- **Orders by Niche**: Which niches are selling best
- **Conversion Rate**: Visitors to Sales

### Channel Metrics

#### Etsy
- **Listings**: Total active listings (Target: 50 by end of Month 1)
- **Views**: Total product views
- **Favorites**: Total favorites/wishlist adds
- **Sales**: Total orders
- **Revenue**: Total revenue from Etsy
- **Search Ranking**: Position for key search terms
- **Feedback Score**: Seller rating

#### Instagram
- **Followers**: Total followers (Target: 700-1000 by end of Month 1)
- **Posts**: Total posts published
- **Reels**: Total Reels published
- **Engagement Rate**: (Likes + Comments) / Followers
- **Reach**: Total unique accounts reached
- **Impressions**: Total post impressions
- **Clicks**: Clicks to website/Etsy
- **Saves**: Total post saves

#### Facebook
- **Page Followers**: Total page followers
- **Marketplace Listings**: Active listings (Target: 80+ by end of Month 1)
- **Engagement**: Likes, comments, shares
- **Reach**: Total unique accounts reached
- **Clicks**: Clicks to website/Etsy
- **Messages**: Customer inquiries

#### Website (kuchiink.com)
- **Traffic**: Total monthly visitors
- **Page Views**: Total page views
- **Bounce Rate**: Percentage of single-page sessions
- **Average Session Duration**: Time spent on site
- **Conversion Rate**: Visitors to sales
- **Top Pages**: Most visited pages
- **Traffic Sources**: Referral sources (Etsy, Instagram, Pinterest, etc.)

#### Pinterest
- **Followers**: Total followers
- **Pins**: Total pins published (Target: 280 by end of Month 1)
- **Monthly Viewers**: Total monthly viewers
- **Outbound Clicks**: Clicks to Etsy/website
- **Click-Through Rate**: Clicks / Impressions
- **Top Pins**: Most clicked pins
- **Top Boards**: Most active boards

### Customer Metrics
- **Total Customers**: Unique customers
- **Repeat Customers**: Customers with 2+ orders
- **Customer Acquisition Cost**: Marketing spend / New customers
- **Customer Lifetime Value**: Average revenue per customer
- **Customer Satisfaction**: Feedback scores, reviews

### Financial Metrics

#### Revenue
- **Total Revenue**: All channels combined
- **Revenue by Channel**: Etsy, Instagram, Facebook, Website, Pinterest
- **Revenue by Niche**: Which niches generate most revenue
- **Monthly Revenue**: Cumulative monthly revenue
- **Revenue Growth**: Month-over-month growth rate

#### Costs
- **Etsy Fees**: Listing fees + transaction fees
- **Supplier Costs**: Product costs from Shubhovastra
- **Shipping Costs**: Shipping expenses
- **Marketing Costs**: Ads, influencer partnerships (if any)
- **Total Costs**: All expenses
- **Profit Margin**: (Revenue - Costs) / Revenue

#### Profitability
- **Gross Profit**: Revenue - COGS
- **Net Profit**: Revenue - All Costs
- **Profit Margin**: Net Profit / Revenue
- **ROI**: (Profit / Investment) × 100

## Tracking Tools

### Free Tools Stack
1. **Etsy Analytics**: Native Etsy dashboard
   - Track listings, views, sales, revenue
   - Monitor search rankings
   - View customer feedback

2. **Google Analytics 4**: Website traffic
   - Set up GA4 on website
   - Track visitors, page views, conversions
   - Monitor traffic sources

3. **Instagram Insights**: Native Instagram analytics
   - Track followers, engagement, reach
   - Monitor post performance
   - View audience demographics

4. **Facebook Analytics**: Native Facebook analytics
   - Track page followers, engagement
   - Monitor marketplace listings
   - View audience insights

5. **Pinterest Analytics**: Native Pinterest analytics
   - Track followers, monthly viewers
   - Monitor pin performance
   - View outbound clicks

6. **Airtable**: Centralized database
   - Create master tracking sheet
   - Link all channel data
   - Create automated reports

## Automation & Self-Improvement Loop

### Weekly Automated Tasks
1. **Design Analysis**: Review best-performing designs
2. **Niche Performance**: Identify top-performing niches
3. **Content Optimization**: Refresh Etsy tags based on search trends
4. **Social Media Optimization**: Adjust Instagram content type based on engagement
5. **Trend Monitoring**: Scan Google Trends and Pinterest Trends for new design ideas
6. **Email Campaign**: Send weekly newsletter to subscriber list

### Monthly Automated Tasks
1. **Financial Review**: Calculate profit, ROI, cost per acquisition
2. **Customer Analysis**: Segment customers, identify patterns
3. **Competitive Analysis**: Monitor competitor pricing and designs
4. **Inventory Planning**: Plan next month's design batch
5. **Strategy Adjustment**: Update strategies based on performance data

## Tools Integration

### Data Flow
1. **Etsy** → Etsy Analytics → Airtable (manual weekly export)
2. **Instagram** → Instagram Insights → Airtable (manual weekly export)
3. **Facebook** → Facebook Analytics → Airtable (manual weekly export)
4. **Website** → Google Analytics → Airtable (manual weekly export)
5. **Pinterest** → Pinterest Analytics → Airtable (manual weekly export)
6. **Airtable** → Automated Reports → Dashboard View

### Dashboard View
- Real-time summary cards (followers, sales, revenue)
- Weekly trend charts (sales, revenue, engagement)
- Channel comparison table
- Top designs leaderboard
- Financial summary
- Action items list
