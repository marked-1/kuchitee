# KuchiTee Pinterest Strategy - Month 1 Launch

## Pinterest Goals
- **Target**: 10 pins/day (70 pins/week)
- **Boards**: 10 boards (8 niches + 1 trending + 1 general)
- **Links**: All pins link to Etsy or website

## Pin Strategy

### Pin Format
- **Vertical format**: 1000x1500px (optimal for Pinterest)
- **Design**: Product mockup + niche-specific background + bold text overlay
- **Text**: Pin title (40-60 characters) + niche indicator
- **Colors**: High-contrast, eye-catching, aligned with niche aesthetic

### Pin Creation Tips
1. Use product mockup images (already generated)
2. Add text overlay with design name and niche
3. Use trending colors and fonts
4. Include call-to-action ("Shop Now", "Get Yours", etc.)
5. Maintain consistent branding

## Pin Optimization

### Keywords in Descriptions
